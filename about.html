Assamese Thali

Things to know about Assamese Thali

An Assamese thali is served in bell metal utensils. Bell metal is an alloy of copper and tin. Bell metal utensils are used by Assamese communities for domestic and religious purposes. In an Assamese thali bell metal plate, bowls, glass etc are used which makes Assamese thali unique from other Indian thalis. (Source : RumiCooks)
